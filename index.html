Getting Started

Made for ProctorU, By Everyone

The ProctorU Hedwig Design System includes the resources to create user interfaces consistent with the ProctorU principles, design language, and best practices. Rather than focusing on pixels, engineers can focus on application logic, while designers can focus on user experience, interactions, and flows. Other teams can use this system to get further along in presentations and client facing documents.

How to Use these Resources

Engineering

Familiarize yourself with the following:

  1. Get an overview of our Markup and Style guidelines including the class naming conventions used in our CSS.
  2. Review the Components; each component provides semantically correct and accessible markup and documentation.

Design

Start with the following:

  1. Explore the Guidelines to learn the ProctorU product design patterns and principles.
  2. Review the Components section, to familiarize yourself with the existing components which you can incorporate into your designs.

Other Teams

These resources are designed to be used for all teams at ProctorU not just engineering, product, and design. There are resources to help you in your day-to-day communication as well as design elements and document templates for you to use when you need a starting point. Here a few resources:

  • Branding Guidelines
  • Voice and Tone Guidelines
  • Sample Documents
  • Messaging Templates
