Buttons

Buttons should follow these general guidelines

  • Accessibility should always be a top consideration. Text color and link color pass WCAG AAA standards for color contrast
  • Text should always be center aligned
  • Border-radius should at least
  • Buttons can be in any of the Brand colors
  • Alerts should have a minimum of 8px of internal padding
